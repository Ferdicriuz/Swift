Privacy and Policy

Buyer Seller Escrow

Buyer

Scope of Data Collection

We collect, process, and store data from buyers to enhance their experience on the platform. The types of data collected include:

  • Financial Information: Securely encrypted payment details such as credit/debit card numbers or third-party wallet information.
  • Behavioural Data: Search history, purchase patterns, product preferences, and interactions on the platform.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, browser type, operating system, and geolocation data.
  • Communication Preferences: Opt-in/opt-out preferences for marketing emails, notifications, and promotions.

Purpose of Data Usage

We use buyer data for:

  • Seamless Transactions: Ensuring efficient checkout and payment processes.
  • Tailored Experiences: Delivering personalized product recommendations and curated shopping experiences based on preferences and past interactions.
  • Fraud Prevention: Detecting and mitigating fraudulent activities or unauthorized access.
  • Customer Support: Resolving buyer issues efficiently, including order disputes and returns.
  • Legal Compliance: Adhering to applicable laws, such as GDPR and CCPA, for buyer data protection.
  • Data Analytics: Using aggregated insights to enhance platform features and user experience.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • Data is shared only with vetted third parties, including payment processors, delivery partners, and fraud detection services.
  • Aggregated and anonymized data may be used for analytics and improving platform performance.
  • Buyer data will never be sold or shared for unauthorized marketing purposes.
  • Data sharing will strictly adhere to GDPR, CCPA, or other relevant regulatory frameworks.

Data Security

We implement encryption, secure servers, and regular audits to protect buyer data.

Data Retention

  • Data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose or comply with legal obligations.
  • Buyers may request data deletion, which will be processed within 30 days unless restricted by legal requirements.
  • Archived transaction data may be retained for up to 7 years for auditing and legal purposes.

Buyer Rights

  • Right to access and rectify personal data.
  • Right to data portability and deletion.
  • Right to restrict or object to specific data processing practices.
  • Right to withdraw consent for data processing where applicable.
  • Buyers can manage these rights via account settings or contacting our data protection team.

Seller

Scope of Data Collection

We gather essential information to ensure sellers can manage their accounts and conduct business effectively on the platform:

  • Business Information: Registered business name, address, and tax identification numbers.
  • Contact Information: Email addresses, phone numbers, and authorized representatives’ details.
  • Banking Information: Securely processed payout details for timely disbursement.
  • Product Listings: Images, descriptions, pricing, and inventory data uploaded by sellers.
  • Communication Preferences: Opt-in/opt-out preferences for marketing emails, notifications, and promotions.

Purpose of Data Usage

  • Marketplace Operations: Facilitating product listings, seller-buyer interactions, and order fulfillment.
  • Performance Metrics: Providing sellers with insights into their sales trends, customer feedback, and marketplace rankings.
  • Fraud Detection: Monitoring for counterfeit goods, fake listings, or unauthorized activities.
  • Compliance Reporting: Ensuring seller activities comply with legal and tax regulations.

Data Security Measure

  • Strict encryption protocols for sensitive data, such as banking and tax information.
  • Role-based access control to prevent unauthorized access to seller data.
  • Regular audits and penetration testing to ensure the integrity of seller data systems.

Data Sharing Policies

  • Limited to trusted partners, such as payment gateways and fraud prevention agencies.
  • Product information will be publicly visible to buyers but does not include sensitive seller data.
  • Regulatory data sharing, when required, is handled in strict compliance with relevant laws.

Data Retention

  • Seller data will be retained for the duration of the account’s active status.Archived data is kept for regulatory compliance for up to 7 years post-account closure.
  • Data deletion requests from sellers are subject to compliance with financial and tax auditing requirements.

Escrow Payment

Overview

The escrow payment system ensures secure transactions by holding funds in a neutral third-party account until the buyer confirms satisfaction with the purchased item.

Data Collected

  • To ensure a seamless and secure flow of funds between buyers and sellers.
  • To provide transparency and accountability in case of disputes.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory frameworks for financial transactions.

Data Usage

  • To ensure a seamless and secure flow of funds between buyers and sellers.
  • To provide transparency and accountability in case of disputes.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory frameworks for financial transactions.

Conflict Resolution

  • Buyers and sellers must provide supporting evidence for disputes.
  • Funds will be released based on the outcome of the platform’s resolution process.
  • Arbitration records are maintained for a period of 5 years.

Security Measures

  • Payments are processed using PCI-DSS-compliant systems to ensure data protection.
  • Multi-factor authentication for all payment activities.
  • End-to-end encryption for all escrow communications and transactions.
