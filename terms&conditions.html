Buyer Seller Escrow

Buyer

General Obligations

  • Buyers must provide accurate and complete information during registration and checkout.
  • Buyers agree to abide by all platform policies, including fair usage and ethical behaviour.
  • Buyers are responsible for ensuring their account security and reporting any suspicious activities.

Purchase Policy

  • Buyers must verify the accuracy of product descriptions and terms before completing a purchase.
  • Orders are binding once placed, with cancellation governed by the seller’s specific policies.
  • Buyers agree to comply with all applicable local, national, or international purchase regulations.

Returns and Refunds

  • Refund eligibility is subject to the seller’s return policy and platform guidelines.
  • Refunds for escrow transactions are processed only after resolution of disputes, if applicable.
  • Buyers must initiate refund or return requests within the stipulated timeframe as per the seller’s policy.

Prohibited Activities

  • Using fraudulent payment methods.
  • Engaging in abusive or defamatory communication with sellers or platform staff.
  • Attempting to bypass escrow mechanisms or commit fraud.
  • Posting false reviews or engaging in malicious behavior to harm sellers or competitors.

Platform Limitations

  • The platform is not liable for damages exceeding the transaction value.
  • Buyers acknowledge that the platform does not own or directly sell products.
  • Buyers agree to resolve disputes through the platform’s formal dispute resolution mechanism.

Seller

Account Requirements

  • Sellers must undergo identity and business verification to operate on the platform.
  • Sellers are responsible for ensuring compliance with local laws and taxes.
  • Sellers must maintain the confidentiality of their account credentials and report breaches immediately.

Product Listings

  • Sellers must provide accurate, non-deceptive product information.
  • Counterfeit or prohibited items are strictly forbidden.
  • Sellers must update inventory status promptly to avoid buyer inconvenience.

Fulfillment and Shipping

  • Orders must be fulfilled within the timeframes specified in the product listing.
  • Sellers are liable for any shipping delays caused by their negligence.
  • Sellers must provide tracking information to buyers for shipped orders.

Dispute Resolution

  • Sellers must respond to disputes promptly and provide necessary evidence.
  • The platform’s decision in disputes is final and binding.
  • Sellers must comply with refund or replacement obligations if proven at fault.

Fees and Payouts

  • Transaction fees are deducted before payout.
  • Payouts are processed only after buyer confirmation or resolution of disputes.
  • Sellers agree to any additional charges for optional premium services, such as advertising or analytics.

Additional Policies

    • Gamification and Loyalty

    • Buyers can participate in gamified activities, earning loyalty points for purchases or platform engagements.
    • Points have an expiration date and are subject to terms.
    • Sellers may offer buyer-specific promotions to drive engagement.

    Customer Support

    • Available 24/7 via email, chat, or phone.
    • Complex issues may take up to 10 business days for resolution.
    • A dedicated escalation channel is available for unresolved disputes.

    Security and Fraud Prevention

    • Multi-layer authentication for buyer and seller accounts.
    • AI-driven monitoring of transactions to identify and mitigate fraudulent activities.
    • Real-time alerts for suspicious login attempts or unusual transaction activity.
